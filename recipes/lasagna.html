Lasagna

Description

Layers of pure comfort, lasagna is the perfect balance of rich flavors and satisfying textures. Velvety pasta sheets embrace a savory filling of seasoned meat, creamy ricotta, and tangy marinara sauce, all melted together with golden, bubbly cheese. Every bite is a warm, indulgent experience that brings together classic Italian tradition and homemade love.

Whether it's a cozy family dinner or a special gathering, lasagna is a dish that never fails to impress. Its hearty layers create a perfect harmony of flavors, making it both a crowd-pleaser and a timeless favorite. Simple yet irresistible, this oven-baked masterpiece is comfort food at its finest.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.

Alfredo Sauce

Description

Smooth, creamy, and irresistibly rich, Alfredo sauce is the ultimate indulgence. Made with butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese, it coats every bite of pasta in a luscious, velvety embrace. Its delicate balance of savory, garlicky warmth and cheesy goodness creates a luxurious flavor that feels both comforting and elegant.

Perfect for fettuccine, chicken, or even a vegetable medley, Alfredo sauce turns any dish into a restaurant-worthy experience. Whether enjoyed as a cozy homemade meal or a special dinner treat, its creamy perfection makes every bite unforgettable. Simple, yet decadently deliciousâ€”it's a sauce that always satisfies.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Melt the butter, then stir in the cream and simmer.
  2. Whisk in the cheese and garlic until heated through and smooth.
  3. Stir in the parsley.
